# SLAMBERZ — Cap Series Design
> Flavour, names, and effect profiles for cap series.
> Series identity: strong visual theme + coherent effect profile + 90s nostalgia energy.
> IP rule: satirical reference is fine, direct copy is not.

## SERIES 1 — Raptor Strike Squad
*Already exists in repo. Power Rangers parody — dino-mecha-warriors in colour-coded armour.*
*Effect profile: pure scoring backbone, common rarity, reliable baseline.*

16 caps, one per colour/mech variant. No special effects — these are the Standard Caps of the game. The dependable foundation every stack is built on.

## SERIES 2 — 8-Ball Blitz
*Pool / billiards theme. 16 caps: cue ball + 15 numbered balls.*
*Effect profile: number-conditional synergies. The series rewards owning more of the set.*

### The caps

| # | Name | Colour | Rarity | Effect |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| 0 | **Cue Ball** | White | Uncommon | Face-up: if every other face-up cap this throw has a number, flip all face-down caps to face-up |
| 1 | **Solid 1** | Yellow solid | Common | Face-up: +1 score |
| 2 | **Solid 2** | Blue solid | Common | Face-up: +1 score |
| 3 | **Solid 3** | Red solid | Common | Face-up: +1 score |
| 4 | **Solid 4** | Purple solid | Common | Face-up: +1 score |
| 5 | **Solid 5** | Orange solid | Common | Face-up: +1 score |
| 6 | **Solid 6** | Green solid | Common | Face-up: +1 score |
| 7 | **Solid 7** | Maroon solid | Common | Face-up: +1 score |
| 8 | **Eight Ball** | Black | Rare | Face-up: 2× to all other face-up caps. Face-down: −3★ (the curse of the eight ball) |
| 9 | **Stripe 9** | Yellow stripe | Common | Face-up: +2 score |
| 10 | **Stripe 10** | Blue stripe | Common | Face-up: +2 score |
| 11 | **Stripe 11** | Red stripe | Common | Face-up: +2 score |
| 12 | **Stripe 12** | Purple stripe | Common | Face-up: +2 score |
| 13 | **Stripe 13** | Orange stripe | Common | Face-up: +2 score |
| 14 | **Stripe 14** | Green stripe | Common | Face-up: +2 score |
| 15 | **Stripe 15** | Maroon stripe | Common | Face-up: +2 score |

### Synergies

**Solids vs Stripes split** — if your throw has more solids face-up than stripes (or vice versa), the Eight Ball gives 3× instead of 2×. Rewards specialising into one half of the set.

**Full rack** — if all 15 numbered caps are in your collection (not necessarily all drawn), the Cue Ball's effect upgrades: instead of flipping face-down caps, it gives +1★ per numbered cap currently face-up. With a full rack and a lucky throw this can be the highest single-throw scorer in the game.

**Corner pocket** — if caps 1, 8, and 15 all land face-up in the same throw: 3× multiplier to everything. Flavour: you sank the corners and the eight ball.

**Suggested set bonus relic** *(acquired via Mystery Bag or relic node if player owns 8+ 8-Ball caps)*:
> *"Rack 'Em Up"* — at the start of each node, one random face-down cap in your collection is temporarily treated as face-up for draw purposes (i.e. always drawn this node).

## SERIES 3 — Dino Detonators
*TMNT / Street Sharks parody — but dinosaurs, in the sewers, with explosives.*
*These are not noble warrior dinosaurs. They are chaotic gremlin dinosaurs who found a munitions depot.*
*Effect profile: volatile, destructive, high-risk/high-reward. Mostly Category E with some utility.*

### The 16 caps

| # | Name | Dino type | Vibe | Effect |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | **Kaboom Rex** | T-Rex | Strapped with TNT, tiny arms holding detonator | Face-up: destroys the nearest nearby cap — remaining caps score 2× *(Dynamite Cap)* |
| 2 | **Sewer Stomper** | Brachiosaurus | Neck too long for the sewer, head stuck in manhole | Face-up: +3 score. Face-down: knocks one random nearby cap to face-down too (splash damage) |
| 3 | **Shred Claw** | Velociraptor | Wearing a backwards cap and rollerskates | Face-up: if exactly one nearby cap — 5× to that cap and this one *(Solo Cap)* |
| 4 | **Blasto-saurus** | Ankylosaurus | Tail replaced with a wrecking ball made of C4 | Face-up: 2× to everything — destroys itself if more than half the stack lands face-down *(Glass Cap)* |
| 5 | **Gunk Lord** | Stegosaurus | Plates replaced with toxic waste barrels | Face-up: all nearby caps gain +2 bonus. Also adds Parasite enchant to one random nearby cap for this throw only |
| 6 | **Crunch McGee** | Triceratops | Three horns, each with a different grenade on it | Face-up: destroys itself — but gives +5★ directly to score and adds one random card to hand |
| 7 | **Pizza Ptero** | Pterodactyl | Dive-bombing with a pizza box full of smoke grenades | Face-up: re-triggers the effect of one random cap from the previous throw *(Echo Cap)* |
| 8 | **The Mole** | Pachycephalosaurus | Head-butting through the sewer floor, hard hat on | Face-up: 10× to itself per nearby cap — multiplies those nearby caps by 0 *(Black Hole Cap)* |
| 9 | **Swamp Breath** | Spinosaurus | Sail fin replaced with a chemical plant chimney | Face-up: all caps within close neighbour radius gain Negative enchant for this throw — but score 3× |
| 10 | **Nitro Neck** | Diplodocus | Extremely long neck used as a battering ram | Face-up: +1 bonus to all caps it physically flew past (proximity to slammer impact zone) |
| 11 | **Screech** | Dilophosaurus | Frill out, spitting green goo at everything | Face-up: one random nearby cap's effect is copied and triggers twice this throw |
| 12 | **Barrel Roll** | Doedicurus (armadillo-dino) | Rolled into a ball, covered in spikes | Face-up: consumes one extra throw — gives 2× multiplier per throw consumed *(Hourglass Cap)* |
| 13 | **Big Mama** | Mama T-Rex | Carrying babies who are also carrying explosives | Face-up: very high flat score — destroys itself afterwards *(Jackpot Cap)* |
| 14 | **Sludge Foot** | Iguanodon | Standing in a puddle of radioactive goo, loving it | Face-up: all face-up caps in this throw gain +1 permanent bonus for the rest of the run |
| 15 | **Colonel Claw** | Raptor in military uniform | Medals, beret, pointing dramatically at nothing | Face-up: 3× multiplier to all other face-up caps with the same base score as this one *(Dada Cap)* |
| 16 | **DINO-MITE** | Unknown species, mostly explosion | Just an explosion with feet | Face-up: destroys every cap within close neighbour radius including itself — all surviving caps score 4× |

### Series identity notes
- **Rarity distribution:** 8 Common, 5 Uncommon (Kaboom Rex, The Mole, Barrel Roll, Big Mama, DINO-MITE), 3 Rare (Blasto-saurus, Sludge Foot, DINO-MITE)
- **DINO-MITE** is the chase card of the series — Rare, volatile, hilarious
- The series skews chaotic. A full Dino Detonators stack is unpredictable but can explode into huge scores
- Visual language: sewer greens and browns, neon toxic colours, retrofuturist comic-book explosions
- **Slammer suggestion:** *"The Drill Sergeant"* — a raptor-headed slammer with a military helmet. Higher `strengthMult`, lower `precisionMult`. Goes hard, goes sideways.

## SERIES 4 — Cosmic Chaos
*90s alien/UFO aesthetic — the kind on lunchboxes and skateboards, not the scary kind.*
*Effect profile: proximity and area effects. Caps that care about what's around them.*

### Concept
16 caps across four factions: **Greys** (classic aliens), **Void** (black hole/space horror lite), **Nebula** (cosmic energy), **Probe** (UFO technology). Each faction has a distinct effect flavour.

### Faction breakdown

**Greys** (4 caps) — curious, harmless, copy-cat effects
- *Zorp* — Common. Face-up: copies the score of the nearest other face-up cap
- *Blorp* — Common. Face-up: +1 bonus to all nearby caps
- *The Examiner* — Uncommon. Face-up: stores the effect of the last cap it landed next to, triggers it this throw
- *Hive Mind* — Rare. Face-up: all Greys in this throw activate twice

**Void** (4 caps) — destructive, greedy
- *Singularity* — Uncommon. Face-up: 10× to itself per nearby cap — multiplies those nearby caps by 0 (the Black Hole Cap, cosmic edition)
- *Event Horizon* — Uncommon. Face-up: destroys nearest cap — surviving caps score 2×
- *Dark Matter* — Rare. Face-up: 5× multiplier to everything. Face-down: −5★
- *The Nothing* — Common. Face-up: +0 score. Counts as face-up for all proximity calculations (invisible anchor)

**Nebula** (4 caps) — amplifiers, energy transfer
- *Stardust* — Common. Face-up: +2 bonus to all close neighbour caps
- *Solar Flare* — Uncommon. Face-up: 2× to all face-up caps within nearby radius
- *Pulsar* — Uncommon. Face-up: triggers once per throw per other face-up cap within close neighbour radius
- *Supernova* — Rare. Face-up: 3× to everything — destroys itself. If it was the last throw, does not destroy itself

**Probe** (4 caps) — utility, manipulation
- *Crop Circle* — Common. Face-up: all caps within nearby radius are considered close neighbours for this throw
- *Abduction Beam* — Uncommon. Face-up: one random face-down cap is flipped to face-up
- *The Mothership* — Rare. Face-up: draws one extra cap into the next throw (temporarily exceeds stack size limit)
- *Area 52* — Uncommon. Face-up: score is secret until round end (shown as ??? — revealed as a multiplied surprise)

### Series set bonus
If all four factions are represented in a single throw (at least one Grey, one Void, one Nebula, one Probe, all face-up): **"First Contact"** — 5× multiplier to the entire throw. Rare but devastating when it happens.

## OTHER SERIES IDEAS (sketched, not fully designed)

**"Scary Skullz"** *(already exists)*
Effect profile suggestion: Negative and Fragile enchant territory — edgy, self-destructive, high ceiling. The skull that destroys itself for maximum score is flavourfully perfect here.

**"Dojo Masters"**
Martial arts / ninja theme. 12 caps. Effect profile: precision and conditional — effects trigger only when caps land within very tight proximity. Shuriken cap that destroys a nearby cap. Sensei cap that buffs all students (lower-numbered Dojo caps). Belt-rank system where rarer caps have stronger but more conditional effects.

**"Mutant Lunch Club"**
Vague TMNT energy but with food instead of turtles — sentient mutant fast food that lives in the sewers. Pizza slice, burger, hot dog, taco, all mutated and angry. Pairs naturally with Dino Detonators lore-wise (they live in the same sewers). Effect profile: bonus and support, slightly chaotic.

**"Horoscope Hustle"**
12 caps, one per star sign. Effect profile: random and luck-based, thematically appropriate. Aries always scores high but destroys itself. Libra balances — copies the average score of all other caps. Scorpio poisons nearby caps (Negative enchant). Gemini splits its score between itself and a random other cap. Very 90s mysticism aesthetic — crystals, stars, that Sailor Moon / New Age crossover energy.

**"Total Wipeout FC"**
Fake football club — toxic waste sponsors, players with gas masks, stadium that's clearly on fire. 11 caps (one per position) + 1 manager cap + 1 referee cap + 3 substitute caps = 16. Effect profile: team synergy — caps in the same "formation" (specific proximity arrangements) get bonuses. Manager cap buffs all others. Referee cap can disqualify (discard) a nearby cap but gives +3★ compensation.
