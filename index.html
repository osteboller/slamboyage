How to play

🎯
Goal

Flip as many caps face-up as possible. Caps that land picture-side up count as won.

👆
Aim & power

Tap the field once to throw. Where you tap sets the aim. When you tap (while the power bar swings) sets the power level. Both are decided by the same tap!

Power levels

The power bar swings up and down. Your power level is set by where the bar is when you tap:

  • Green = full power — strong blast, hard to control
  • Yellow = medium power
  • Red = weak blast — good control

Tip: Timing is everything! Tap when the bar is where you want the power level.

🔄
Rounds

You have 3 throws per round. Between throws, caps are restacked automatically — tap to confirm and continue.

⚙
Settings

Use the ⚙ button in the top right to select slammer and adjust physics and cap count.
