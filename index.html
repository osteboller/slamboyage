How to play

🎯
Goal

Flip as many caps face-up as possible. Caps that land picture-side up count as won.

👆
Aim

Hold your finger on the field to aim — a reticle shows where you'll throw. Drag to adjust. The power bar starts swinging the moment you press down.

Power

The power bar swings while you hold. Release when the bar is where you want it — that's when the throw fires:

  • Green = full power — strong blast, hard to control
  • Yellow = medium power
  • Red = weak blast — good control

Tip: Hold to line up your shot, then release at the right moment!

🔄
Rounds

You have 3 throws per round. Between throws, caps are restacked — tap anywhere to confirm and continue.

🎒
Bag

Open the Bag to see your caps, relics and cards — and to equip a different slammer.
